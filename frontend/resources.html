SES Mobile App

Monitor your HVAC system from anywhere with real-time alerts and quick actions.

iOS App

Download from Apple App Store - Search "Save Energy Systems"

Android App

Download from Google Play Store - Search "Save Energy Systems"

Key App Features:

  • Real-time Alerts - Instant push notifications for system issues
  • Temperature Monitoring - View all zones with 24-hour graphs
  • Create Tickets - Report issues with photos and voice notes
  • Energy Dashboard - Track usage and costs in real-time
  • Parts Ordering - In-app catalog with barcode scanner
  • Quick Actions - One-tap emergency calling and support
  • Offline Access - Key information available without connection

First Time Setup:

  1. Download app from App Store or Google Play
  2. Login with same credentials as web portal
  3. Enable push notifications (important for alerts!)
  4. Select your Planet Fitness location as primary site
  5. Enable biometric login (Face ID/Fingerprint) for convenience
  6. Add home screen widget for quick temperature checks

Weather Emergency Protocols

Freezing Temperature

24-hour advance prep, heating system verification, pipe protection, hourly monitoring

Hurricane/Severe Storms

48-hour preparation, equipment protection, shutdown procedures, post-storm inspection

Heat Wave (95°F+ / 3+ Days)

3-day advance check, system optimization, continuous monitoring, member comfort focus

Tornado/Severe Wind

Immediate shelter, weather monitoring, post-event inspection, damage documentation

Power Outage

Utility notification, system shutdown, 5-minute wait before restart, gradual re-energization

Regional Considerations

Climate-specific protocols for northern cold, southern heat, coastal storms

Weather Emergency Contact

Before, during, or after any severe weather event, contact SES for guidance:

Call: (617) 564-4800

Document all weather damage with photos and videos for insurance claims.

Key Weather Protocols:

  • Freezing: Never set back heat below 65°F, monitor pipes hourly, clear snow from outdoor units
  • Heat Wave: Don't lower thermostat below 74°F, close blinds, minimize door openings
  • Hurricane: Follow SES shutdown plan, secure outdoor equipment, document all damage
  • Power Outage: Wait 5 minutes after power returns, call SES before restart, monitor for 30 minutes
