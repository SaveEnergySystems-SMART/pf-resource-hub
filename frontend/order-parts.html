How to Order Parts

1

Identify Part Needed

Check equipment label for model number, note part description, take photo of existing part, and review SES dashboard for recommendations.

2

Access Ordering System

Login to SES Portal with your credentials, navigate to Parts Catalog, or call (617) 564-4800 for assistance.

3

Search and Order

Enter part number or keyword, verify compatibility with your system, check availability and lead time, add to cart and submit.

4

Track Order

Receive confirmation email, track shipment via portal. Standard shipping: 2-3 days. Emergency: Next day available.

Common Replacement Parts

Air Filters

Sizes: 16x20x1, 16x25x1, 20x20x1, 20x25x1
MERV Rating: MERV 8 (standard), MERV 11 (high-eff)
Order Qty: Box of 12 recommended
Replace: Every 30-90 days

Smart Thermostats

Type: Wi-Fi enabled, touchscreen
Batteries: CR2032 (2 per unit)
Compatibility: All SES DLC systems
Install: SES certified tech required

Temperature Sensors

Type: Zone-specific wireless sensors
Battery Life: 2-3 years
Replace: As needed
Install: SES tech recommended

Fan Belts

Types: V-belt, serpentine
Sizes: System-specific
Inspect: Quarterly
Replace: Every 2-3 years

Coil Cleaner

Type: Professional grade, biodegradable
Usage: Quarterly maintenance
Order: Bulk available
Safety: Follow all instructions

Drain Line Parts

Types: Condensate pumps, float switches
Replace: As needed
Maintenance: Quarterly cleaning
Critical: Prevents water damage
SES CERTIFIED TECH ONLY: Control boards, relays, contactors, and capacitors require professional installation. Never attempt electrical work on HVAC systems.
EPA CERTIFIED ONLY: Refrigerant (R-410A, R-32) can only be ordered and handled by EPA certified technicians through SES at (617) 564-4800.

Recommended Emergency Stock

Keep these items on-site for quick response to common issues:

  • Air filters (2-month supply)
  • Thermostat batteries (CR2032 x 10)
  • Fuses (various amp ratings)
  • Condensate drain tablets
  • Basic tools (screwdrivers, flashlight)
  • SES emergency contact card
  • Equipment model/serial number list
  • Parts ordering login credentials
