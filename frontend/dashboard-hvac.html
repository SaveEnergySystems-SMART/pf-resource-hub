My HVAC isn't working - What should I do?

Emergency? If your HVAC is completely out during extreme weather, call 24/7 Emergency Support: 1-800-555-HVAC (4822)
Follow these troubleshooting steps:
  1. Check the Thermostat:
    • Is it set to the correct mode (Heat/Cool)?
    • Is the temperature set correctly?
    • Are the batteries working? (if applicable)
  2. Check the Circuit Breaker:
    • Go to your electrical panel
    • Look for tripped breakers labeled "HVAC" or "AC"
    • Flip it off, then back on
  3. Check the Air Filter:
    • A clogged filter can cause the system to shut down
    • Replace if dirty (order new filters under "Order Parts")
  4. Check the Outdoor Unit:
    • Is it clear of debris, leaves, or snow?
    • Is the fan spinning?
