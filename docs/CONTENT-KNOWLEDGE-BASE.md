## ðŸ“š HVAC TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE

### Common Issue #1: System Not Turning On

**Quick Checks:**
1. **Thermostat Settings**
   - Verify thermostat is set to correct mode (Heat/Cool/Auto)
   - Check temperature setpoint is appropriate
   - Replace batteries if applicable
   - Ensure thermostat display is lit

2. **Circuit Breaker**
   - Locate electrical panel
   - Find breakers labeled "HVAC," "AC," or "Furnace"
   - Check if breaker is tripped (switch in middle position)
   - Reset: flip OFF completely, then back to ON
   - If breaker trips again immediately, call for service

3. **Emergency Shut-Off Switch**
   - Check wall switch near indoor unit (looks like light switch)
   - Ensure it's in ON position
   - Often accidentally turned off during cleaning

**When to Call Support:**
- Breaker trips repeatedly
- Burning smell detected
- Strange noises (grinding, squealing, banging)
- Water leaking from unit

### Common Issue #2: Poor Heating or Cooling

**Causes & Solutions:**

1. **Dirty Air Filter** (Most Common - 40% of issues)
   - Replace every 30-90 days depending on usage
   - Clogged filters reduce airflow by up to 50%
   - Can cause system to overheat and shut down
   - **Action**: Check filter monthly, replace when dirty

2. **Blocked Vents or Registers**
   - Ensure all supply vents are open
   - Remove furniture/equipment blocking airflow
   - Check return air grilles are unobstructed

3. **Refrigerant Issues**
   - Low refrigerant = poor cooling performance
   - Signs: Ice buildup on outdoor unit, hissing sounds
   - **Requires professional service** - do not DIY

4. **Thermostat Placement**
   - Should not be in direct sunlight
   - Keep away from heat sources (gym equipment, windows)
   - Calibration may drift over time

### Common Issue #3: Uneven Temperature Distribution

**Troubleshooting:**
- Check all zone dampers are functioning
- Verify airflow at all registers
- Inspect ductwork for leaks (common in older facilities)
- Consider SES 24-hour temperature monitoring service

## ðŸ“Š QUICK REFERENCE: COMMON HVAC TERMS

- **HVAC**: Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning
- **MERV Rating**: Minimum Efficiency Reporting Value (filter quality: 1-16)
- **Refrigerant**: Chemical that absorbs/releases heat in cooling cycle
- **Tonnage**: Cooling capacity (1 ton = 12,000 BTU/hour)
- **SEER**: Seasonal Energy Efficiency Ratio (higher is better)
- **Thermostat**: Controls temperature settings
- **Compressor**: Heart of AC system, pumps refrigerant
- **Condenser**: Outdoor unit that releases heat
- **Evaporator**: Indoor unit that absorbs heat
- **Air Handler**: Indoor unit that circulates air
