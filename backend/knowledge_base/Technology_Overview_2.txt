WHAT IS SMARTPM PLUS™?

SmartPM Plus™ is a complete AI-driven predictive maintenance and energy 
management solution that works alongside your existing PM program. It combines 
advanced DLC (Demand Limiting Controller) technology with 24/7 managed services 
to deliver comprehensive HVAC oversight, predictive maintenance, and immediate 
energy savings.

KEY DIFFERENTIATORS:
• Works WITH existing PM programs (not a replacement)
• Complete managed service (not just hardware)
• Predictive insights catch failures before they happen
• 24/7 monitoring with Virtual Facility Management (vFM™)
• Full asset tracking through SmartAssetCare™
• 20-30% energy savings from Day 1
• $0 upfront cost (True OPEX model)

COMMON QUESTIONS & ANSWERS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Q: What is SmartPM Plus™?
A: SmartPM Plus™ is an AI-driven predictive maintenance and energy management 
solution that works alongside your existing PM program. It provides 24/7 
monitoring, predictive insights, and delivers 20-30% energy savings from Day 1.

Q: How much does it cost?
A: $0 upfront. SmartPM Plus™ uses your existing PM budget - it's not a new 
line item. Quarterly OPEX invoicing. 36-month partnership with 12-month exit 
clause if value isn't delivered.

Q: Do I need to replace my current PM vendor?
A: No! SmartPM Plus™ works WITH your existing PM program. You can keep your 
current vendor and we add intelligence, OR we can take over PM services - your choice.

Q: What energy savings can I expect?
A: 20-30% HVAC energy savings starting Day 1. Proven across 600+ locations 
nationwide with $2.5M+ in verified client savings.

Q: What if it doesn't work for my facility?
A: 36-month partnership with 12-month exit clause. If energy savings or FM 
value aren't delivered within 12 months, you can cancel - no questions asked.

Q: How long does installation take?
A: 4-6 hours per site. Same-day wireless installation with no HVAC downtime 
and minimal disruption to your operations.

Q: What's included in the service?
A: Per RTU: Smart thermostat, zone sensor, supply sensor, quarterly PM service. 
Plus: vFM™ (24/7 monitoring), SmartAssetCare™ (asset tracking), desktop & 
mobile apps, dedicated support team.

Q: How is this different from traditional PM?
A: SmartPM Plus™ is predictive (not reactive). We catch failures before they 
happen, provide 24/7 monitoring, and deliver immediate energy savings. 
Traditional PM waits for breakdowns and provides no energy optimization.
